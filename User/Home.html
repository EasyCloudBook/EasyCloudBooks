Dashboard

Dashboard is the information page that a user can see just after his/her login. This page contains various information regarding user's project progress as per the roles he is associated with the project. Dashboard will vary its information as per user. There are total 10 informational gadgets shown on this page which are Project Deviation, Due Date, Project Completed, Top 10 Project Tags, Top 10 Account Tags, Top 10 Company Tags, Top 10 Contact Tags, Pending Project By Project Managers, Project Status Today, Accounts Status Today.

What is a Dashboard ?

A dashboard is a user interface that organizes and presents information in a way that is easy to understand.

How to reset your prioritized gadgets ?

step 1: On the dashboard page click on the EDIT GADGETS button. You will be redirected to next page where you can reset your prioritized gadgets.

step 2: After click on the EDIT GADGETS button, here you will find a RESET GADGET ORDERS button as shown with arrowhead. Click that button to reset the order of your gadgets to default.

How to set visibility of your gadgets ?

step 1: On the dashboard page click on the EDIT GADGETS button. you will be redirected to next page where you can set visibility of your gadget to on or off.

step 2: After clicking on the EDIT GADGETS button, here you will find a switch as shown with arrowhead. Switch on/off to set the visibility of your gadget on the dashboard.
